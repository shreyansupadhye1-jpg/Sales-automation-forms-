Why we reached out

We're here to guide you.

Our goal is to guide you in your journey of decorating your space. Most clients come to us not knowing where to start. That's exactly where we begin.

Our process

Here's what happens next.

Step 1 — We listen first.
In our first meeting, we ask about your space, how you live in it, your timeline, and your budget. Just an honest conversation to understand your requirements.
Discovery meeting
Step 2 — We craft a layout for your space.
Based on what we hear, we prepare a scaled furniture layout — a drawing showing how your rooms could actually work. This gives us a concrete starting point to react to, refine, and build from.
Layout plan
Step 3 — We build a concept presentation.
We put together a visual direction for your home — flooring, colours, furniture style, room-by-room concepts. Tailored to your taste, not a generic template.
Concept presentation
Step 4 — We give you a rough project estimate.
We prepare a preliminary BOQ with room-wise, trade-wise cost breakdowns — so you have a clear, honest sense of what your project will cost before committing to anything.
Rough estimate / BOQ
